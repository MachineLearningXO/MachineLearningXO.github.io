Welcome to our Machine Learning Game

Here you will find our Machine Learning TicTacToe and Gomoku which was made as part of our third year project at the University of Kent.

Tic Tac Toe instructions:
The purpose: To achieve 3 of your character (X/O) in a row whether it be diagonal, horizontal or vertical.
How to play: Start by selecting an available square, wait for your opponent and repeat the process until you win, draw or lose.
Gomoku instructions:
The purpose: To achieve 5 of your character (X/O) in a row whether it be diagonal, horizontal or vertical.
How to play: Start by selecting an available square, wait for your opponent and repeat the process until you win, draw or lose.
